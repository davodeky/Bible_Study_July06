Intentional Growth‐ The Timing Gap

This Week's Scriptures

  • Esther 4:13-14

Pointers

  • Like Esther, we have in respective ways been called to play role in the plan of God.
  • Esther had been put in that place at that time because God had a job for her to do.
  • So she had to step up to the plate and get to work.
  • We all have a purpose and a calling- the timing could be that writing on the wall.
  • In order to grow, we need to understand the times that we are living in so as to be effective in our ministries.

Discussion Questions

  • In light of the time that we are living in, how do you see God using you as His ambassador here on earth?
  • What role can the church play in this world today?
  • How can we grow as christians and as members of our respective communities and families in such a time where there seems to be limitations coming from all directions?

Biblical Insights

  • Habakkuk 2:2
  • We live in the “ micro-wave” time.
  • We have become too impatient with ourselves and with one another
  • To grow we sometimes have to be patient in waiting ‐ patience is a virtue.
